General information¶

Going through this documentation you will be able to generate a COP time series with individual input ambient temperatures as well as individual temperature sinks.

For open_MODEX a COP time series was generated for each federal state in Germany using the calc_cops() functionality from oemof.thermal for compression heat pumps.

The calc_cops() functionality is a simple calculation for compression heat pumps and chillers based on temperatures for energy system optimizations with oemof.solph.

To determine the COP of a real machine a scale-down factor (the quality grade \({\eta}\)) is applied on the Carnot efficiency:

(1)¶\[COP = \eta \cdot COP_\mathrm{Carnot}\]

with

\[0 \leq \eta \leq 1\]

Further information regarding the COP calculation see Compression heat pump and chiller.
